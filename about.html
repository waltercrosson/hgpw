WELCOME

Welcome to the Hosta Garden Perennials website! We started 10 years ago with a few plants and a simple love for hostas and flowers. Today our inventory has grown to include over 100 varieties of hostas and a large selection of perennials too!


With the latest addition of our greenhouse we are now able to offer more varieties of hostas and flowers than ever! Stop by and check out our inventory today and you will be pleasantly surprised.

Please note that all of our plants are grown organically, without the use of chemical fertilizers, pestisides or other artificial agents!
